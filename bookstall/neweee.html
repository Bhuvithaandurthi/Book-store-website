DEPARTMENT OF EEE RGUKT BASAR

       
The Department of Electrical Engineering (EEE) at Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT) Basar in Telangana, India offers undergraduate and minor degree programs. The department was established in 2010. 
Programs 

    B-Tech: A four-year program that accepts 140 students per year
    Minor degree: An Electrical Engineering minor degree 

Facilities 12 laboratories with modern technology, A dynamic curriculum, and Tech Fest-event ANTAHPRAGNYA to showcase students' technical talent.
