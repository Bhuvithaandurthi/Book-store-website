DEPARTMENT OF ECE RGUKT BASAR

The Department of Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) at Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT) Basar in Telangana, India offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs. 
        Undergraduate program
        
            B-Tech in ECE with an average intake of 240 students 
        
        Subjects include TV Engineering, Digital Communications, Analog Communications, and more 
        
        Postgraduate program 
        
            M-Tech in ECE with advanced technical courses 
        
        
Faculty

        
           
 The department has core faculty members, including Assistant Professors, who have M.Techs from IIT-Kharagpur and IISc BangaloreBangalore 
        
        Some faculty members have areas of interest in machine learning, 5G wireless communications, and IOT
Labs The department has 11 laboratories with dedicated lab staff

Other activities 

The department organizes guest talks, FDPs, and workshops
    The department's vision is to provide technical knowledge and enrich skills, and excel in academics and research
